--- layout: what-is-remoteroid --- Don't let your phone steal your hands from computer.

Imagine you have a lot of works to do with computer. What if your phone doesn't stop ringing with messages from your friend, which requires your immediate response?

From now, you don't need to worry about that. Remoteroid let you control your phone in your computer. So all you need to do is connect remoteroid with your computer and use your phone on your computer just like as you did before.
